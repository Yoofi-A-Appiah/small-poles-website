Platform Modules

Six tools.
One tactical edge.

Every module in Small Poles is designed to turn raw GPL data into actionable fantasy intelligence — from scouting undervalued players to managing your squad under pressure.

01

Tactics Board

Your command center for matchday.

The Tactics Board is where strategy meets execution. View your starting XI in formation, track gameweek performance in real time, and make last-minute captain changes before the deadline locks.

✓

Formation Management

Switch between 4-4-2 and 4-3-3 formations. Smart validation ensures legal squad composition.

✓

Captain Selection

Designate any starting XI player as captain for double points. Change until the deadline.

✓

Gameweek Scores

Full points breakdown per player — goals, assists, clean sheets, cards, and captain bonus.

✓

Bench & Substitution Logic

Bench players earn points automatically when starters don't play. Order matters.

02

Scout Room

Find value before the market does.

Browse the entire GPL player pool with powerful filters. Sort by position, team, price range, form, and availability. The Scout Room surfaces the data you need to make smart transfer decisions.

✓

Multi-Filter Search

Filter by position, GPL team, price range, availability status, and player name — simultaneously.

✓

Form Tracker

Last 5 gameweek scores at a glance. Spot rising performers and avoid declining assets.

✓

Selection Percentage

See what percentage of all managers own each player. Identify differentials and template picks.

✓

Paginated Results

500+ players organized in clean, scannable pages. 50 per page with instant navigation.

03

Squad Builder

15 players. GHS 100M. Your rules.

Build your dream GPL squad within budget constraints. The Squad Builder enforces all composition rules — 2 GK, 5 DEF, 5 MID, 3 ATT — and tracks your remaining budget in real time as you pick.

✓

Budget Enforcement

GHS 100M total budget. Live remaining balance as you add and remove players.

✓

Position Validation

Exactly 2 GK, 5 DEF, 5 MID, 3 ATT. System prevents illegal compositions.

✓

Starting XI + Bench

11 starters and 4 bench players. Bench order determines auto-substitution priority.

✓

Unique Team Name

Brand your squad with a unique team name that appears on leaderboards and in leagues.

04

Intel Feed

Actionable intelligence, categorized.

Injuries, suspensions, fixture changes, and transfer rumors — all filtered by relevance to your squad. The Intel Feed ensures you never miss a critical update before the transfer deadline.

✓

Injury & Suspension Alerts

Real-time updates on player status with expected return dates and severity levels.

✓

My Squad Filter

Toggle to see only updates affecting players in your current squad.

✓

Deadline Reminders

Email notifications 24 hours and 1 hour before the transfer deadline closes.

✓

Match Results Feed

Completed scores with goal scorers, assist providers, and fantasy-relevant events.

05

Player Dossier

Deep-dive statistical profiles.

Every GPL player gets a comprehensive dossier — season stats, price history, form trajectory, upcoming fixture difficulty, and ownership data. Everything you need to evaluate an asset in one view.

✓

Season Statistics

Goals, assists, clean sheets, cards, saves — full performance breakdown by gameweek.

✓

Price History

Track how a player's fantasy price has moved over the season. Maintained for transparency.

✓

Fixture Difficulty Rating

Upcoming opponents rated 1–5 difficulty. Plan your transfers around favorable fixtures.

✓

Availability Status

Available, injured, suspended, or transferred — plus expected return dates when applicable.

Leagues & Rankings

Compete with everyone. Brag to friends.

Create private leagues with custom names and invite friends via unique join codes. Track your position on the global leaderboard. Social competition is the engine that keeps managers coming back every gameweek.

✓

Private Leagues

Create invite-only leagues for up to 20 members. Share an 8-character join code with friends.

✓

Global Rankings

Rank against all platform users with percentile information and points-to-leader gap.

✓

Multi-League Membership

Join as many leagues as you want. Separate standings for each competition.

✓

League Admin Controls

Manage membership, update descriptions, and maintain the competitive integrity of your league.
