Value Proposition

Value for every
stakeholder.

For Fans

Deeper GPL engagement

Strategic gameplay transforms passive viewership into active participation. Compete with friends, analyze players, and have a reason to watch every GPL match — not just your team's games.

For the GPL

Increased viewership & engagement

Fantasy managers watch more matches and follow more teams. Every fixture becomes relevant when your players are on the pitch. Enhanced fan engagement creates additional partnership and revenue opportunities.

For Sponsors

Engaged, data-rich audience

Access to a highly engaged demographic of football enthusiasts aged 18–35. Rich interaction data provides precise targeting for brands reaching Ghana's urban middle class.

For GPL Teams

Revenue & player insights

Partnership revenue streams, individual player performance analytics, and deeper understanding of which players drive fan interest. Data-backed insights for scouting and fan engagement strategies.

Product Roadmap

From GPL beta
to pan-African platform.

Small Poles starts with a focused GPL MVP and expands methodically — validating at each stage before scaling.

NOW — Year 1

GPL MVP Launch

Web-based platform with core fantasy features — player browsing, team creation, scoring, transfers, and private leagues. Targeting 50–100 beta testers with a data-light strategy that fits within free API tiers.

Web App 50–100 Beta Users Core Fantasy Private Leagues Free Tier Data
Year 2–3

Mobile & Growth

Native iOS and Android apps. Enhanced social features and community tools. Begin exploring other African leagues for expansion. Potential B2B partnerships with GPL teams for fan engagement platforms.
