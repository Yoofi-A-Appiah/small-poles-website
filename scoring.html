Strategy Intel

Scoring tactics
that separate winners.

Captain premium fixtures

Your captain choice is worth roughly 30% of your gameweek score. Target players with easy fixtures and recent form — not just big names.

🛡️

Don't ignore defenders

A clean sheet (4 pts) plus appearance (2 pts) gives DEF/GK a solid floor of 6 points. Teams with strong defensive records are goldmines.

Bank free transfers

If you don't need to transfer, consider saving. Two free transfers next gameweek gives you more flexibility without the -4 point hit.

Form over reputation

A ₵5M midfielder on a 5-match scoring streak will outscore a ₵9M star in poor form. Watch the last 3–5 gameweek scores closely.

📅

Plan fixture swings

Look 2–3 gameweeks ahead. Transfer in players facing easy opponents before their price rises from increased demand.

💡

Differential captaincy

If you're chasing a league rival, captain a player they don't own. The ×2 multiplier on a unique pick creates massive rank swings.

Dynamic Pricing

Prices move with
the market.

Player prices update weekly after each gameweek is completed. The algorithm considers multiple factors to ensure prices reflect both real-world performance and fantasy demand. Maximum weekly change is capped at ±20%.
